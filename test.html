Chat Widget Integration Test

This page simulates a client website embedding the chat widget.

Click the launcher in the bottom right to test.

Test Scenarios:

  1. Booking Modal: Click "Book Appointment". Verify it fades in centered with a dark background.
  2. Quick Close: Click the dark background immediately. It should close.
  3. Smart Protection: Open booking again. Click inside the calendar (e.g., select a date). Now click the background. It should NOT close (X button pulses).
